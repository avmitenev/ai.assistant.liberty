**What is Personal Accident cover?**

Accidents can happen to anyone, and their consequences can be emotionally and financially overwhelming. Our Personal Accident cover provides support and financial protection for you and your loved ones during life's unexpected moments.

**What is covered by Personal Accident?**

* **Compensation in the event of Death:** Receive financial support for your family in the tragic event of your death due to an accident, easing their financial burden during a difficult time.
* **Support for Permanent Total Disability:** Benefit from financial assistance in case of severe, life-altering injuries from an accident, such as the loss of limbs or sight, to help maintain your quality of life.
* **Income Protection During Temporary Disability:** Ensure your financial stability with compensation covering lost wages for up to 104 weeks if an accident temporarily prevents you from working and attending to your regular occupation.
* Other benefits are available depending on the selected policy
