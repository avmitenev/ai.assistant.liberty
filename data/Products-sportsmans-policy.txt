## Sportsmans Policy | Liberty Life Assurance

### What is the SportsMans Policy

This policy covers sportsmen to enable them enjoy their game and also offers compensation for their sports equipment against theft or accidental damage.

*   Sports Equipment - Covers the cost of repairing or replacing such equipment
*   Personal Effects - Covers the cost of repairing or replacing such personal effects lost or damaged at the golf course or other sports premises
*   Third Party Liability - Covers legal liability arising in respect to accidents caused by the insured while playing or practicing resulting in:-
    *   Bodily injury to any non-family/ household member of the insured’s or in his service, other than caddies.
    *   Damage to property not linked to the insured family or person in his service.
*   Personal Accident - Covers the insured in the event of sustaining accidental physically visible injury while playing.
*   Hole In One – Covers the costs incurred by the insured as a result of ‘hole-in-one’ during actual play.

### More about the product

**What is a Hole-In-One?**

A hole-in-one refers to a shot taken from the tee to the cup hole with no intervening shots. This occurrence in a competition normally warrants the player to buy drinks for members at the club up to the limit provided in the policy for which he is liable to claim from the insurer.

**Are the golf clubs covered if left in the locker at the golf club?**

The cover is in place for the golf clubs at all times whilst in the club premises to safeguard them against theft and damage.
