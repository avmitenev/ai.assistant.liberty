## Heritage Insurance Announces the Launch of HeriAfya

In a landmark move for the healthcare industry, Heritage is proud to announce the launch of their new private medical insurance cover, 'Heri Afya'. This innovative health insurance solution is tailored to meet the diverse medical needs of Kenyans, ensuring comprehensive coverage and peace of mind.

'Heri Afya', symbolises our commitment to prioritizing the health and well-being of our clients. Developed with a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced in accessing quality healthcare in Kenya, Heri Afya offers an affordable, flexible, and user-friendly insurance solution for individuals and families.
